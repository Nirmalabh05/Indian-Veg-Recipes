Vegetable Cutlet

Serve up these crisp vegetable samosas as a tasty starter or side dish with chutney or sauce. They also make great buffet food for your next party

Ingredients (Serves: 4):

  1. 4 medium Onion finely chopped
  2. 3-4 Green chilli chopped
  3. 2.5 tsp Ginger and garlic finely chopped
  4. 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  5. 2 tsp Coriander powder
  6. 1/2 tsp Fennel powder
  7. 1 tsp Garam masala
  8. 1 - 1.5 tsp Pepper powder
  9. 4.5 - 5 cups Chopped veggies(carrot, beans, cabbage and beetroot)
  10. 2-3 tbsp Coriander leaves chopped
  11. 4 medium Potato
  12. 2 cups Bread crumbs
  13. 3-4 tbsp Plain flour
  14. Oil I used Sunflower oil

Method:

  1. Microwave the chopped veggies at 100 % power for 4-5 mins. You can steam it on stove top also, instead of using MW.
  2. Heat oil in a wide pan. Add the chopped onion and cook till it becomes soft and starts browning. Add chopped ginger, garlic,green chilli and curry leaves. Cook for 2-3 mins or till the raw smell goes.
  3. Add all the masala powders and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 mins. Add the cooked veggies and salt, mix well. Add chopped coriander leaves. Combine and continue to cook for 5 more mins on low flame.
  4. Let the veggie mixture cool completely. Add the mashed potatoes to the veggie mixture and mix well.Make sure there are no lumps. Shape the mixture as per your liking.
  5. Make a medium thick paste with plain flour and water. Mix together the bread crumbs. Dip the shaped cutlets in this batter & roll in bread crumbs.

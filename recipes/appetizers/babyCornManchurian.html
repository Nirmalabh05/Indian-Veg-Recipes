Baby Corn Manchurian

Crispy Baby Corn Manchurian, is a very delicious, spicy and easy recipe - works perfect as appetizer and great for potlucks if you are out of ideas!

Ingredients

    For marination and frying:
  1. 7 Baby Corn
  2. 1 &1/2 Tablespoon Rice Flour
  3. 1 Tablespoon Maida / All Purpose Flour
  4. Salt as per need
  5. 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
  6. 1 Teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper
  7. 1 Teaspoon Garlic Bread Spice Mix / Italian Seasoning
  8. Oil for frying
    9. Additional:
  9. 2 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  10. 6 Garlic cloves,chopped finely
  11. 2 Green Chilies,slit
  12. 2 Sprigs Cilantro,finely chopped
  13. 2 Sprigs Spring Onion,finely chopped
  14. Salt as per need
  15. 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
  16. 1/2 Teaspoon Red Chili Sauce
  17. 1 &1/2 Tablespoon Chili Garlic Sauce
  18. 1/2 Teaspoon Sesame Seeds

Method

  1. Firstly,wash the Baby Corn and dry them with a kitchen towel. Cut each Baby Corn into half or use it as it is as per choice.
  2. Now in a mixing bowl,add all the ingredients mentioned under marination,except oil and water.Mix them well.
  3. Add water little by little to form a smooth paste. The paste should neither be too thick nor too thin or watery.
  4. In a deep frying pan,heat oil. When the oil is hot enough,bring the flame to medium. Coat all the Baby Corn pieces with the batter. Now slowly drop each piece in the hot oil and deep fry them till they are crispy and fried well.
  5. In a heavy bottom pan,add oil. When the oil gets hot,add garlic,green chilies,cilantro. Fry them for a while.
  6. Now add the spring onion whites and toss them for while on high flame.
  7. Now reduce the flame and add salt,pepper powder,chilli sauce,garlic sauce. Mix well together.
  8. Bring the flame back to high and add fried Baby Corn. Toss them on high flame so that they get coated well with the sauce mixture.
  9. Now switch off the flame and garnish it with spring onion greens. Serve hot!

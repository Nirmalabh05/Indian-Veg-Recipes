Barfi

Milk Powder Burfi is a delicious Indian sweet that can be prepared in flat 15 mins. This Diwali enjoy this 15 mins Halwai style Milk Powder Burfi and impress your family with your cooking skills.

Ingredients

  1. 1/3 cup Ghee
  2. 1/2 cup Milk
  3. 2 cups Milk powder
  4. 1 cup Powdered sugar
  5. 6-8 nos cardamom pods
  6. Dry fruits (Almonds, Pistachios)

Method

  1. Start by greasing and lining a pan with parchment paper. Keep this aside. Also chop some dry fruits to garnish. Keep this ready too.
  2. Melt some ghee in the pan on low heat. Once ghee is melted switch off the gas and add milk and milk powder. Switch on the gas and stir to combine (around 2-3 mins)
  3. Once combined, add in the sugar. You will note that the mixture turns thin or runny.
  4. Keep stirring continuously until it thickens and starts leaving the pan around 10-15 mins. After which transfer it to the greased tray. Smooth out the top with the back of the spatula.
  5. Add chopped nuts on top. With the help of back of the bowl press the nuts on top so that it sticks properly. Do remember to grease the bowl with some ghee too. Allow this to set at room temperature for 1.5-2 hours.
  6. Once cold and set remove it from the tray, cut into desired shape and enjoy.

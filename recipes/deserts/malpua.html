Malpua

Malpua is a traditional Indian sweet from Rajasthan which is basically a fried pancake dunked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients

  1. 1 and 1/2 cup Milk Warm
  2. 1/2 cup Khoya Grated
  3. 1 cup Maida
  4. 1 tsp Saunf Coarsely Ground
  5. 2 tsp Sugar
  6. 1 pinch Salt
  7. 1 pinch Baking Powder
  8. Ghee for frying
  9. Almonds
  10. Pistachios
  11. Saffron
    12. For Sugar Syrup:
  12. 1 cup Sugar
  13. 1/4 cup Water
  14. 2-3 Cardamom Ground
  15. 8-10 strands Saffron

Method

  1. Add sugar, cardamom, saffron and water in a pan.
  2. Cook till the syrup is of one string consistency. Keep aside
  3. Mix half of the warm milk and khoya in a bowl.
  4. Mix till khoya is combined.
  5. Add half of the maida and mix to make a smooth paste.
  6. Add the remaining maida and make a smooth paste.
  7. Now add sugar, saunf, salt and baking powder. Mix well.
  8. Add the remaining milk and mix well.
  9. Keep aside for 10 minutes.
  10. Heat ghee in a shallow pan.
  11. Mix the batter and pour a small ladle full ( approx 2 tbsp ) in the hot ghee. ( The ghee should not be too hot )
  12. Simmer the heat to low and fry the malpua on both the sides till golden brown.
  13. Remove from ghee and dip in the sugar syrup for 2 minutes.
  14. Serve hot as such or with kesar rabdi.

Butter Paneer Masala

Butter Paneer Masala is one of the most popular vegetarian dishes from Indian cuisine. Indian cottage cheese in smooth, creamy and delicious restaurant style gravy.

Ingredients

  1. 1 tablespoon butter
  2. 2 cups paneer (cut into bite sized cubes)
  3. 1 tablespoon butter
  4. 1 onion (pureed)
  5. 1 teaspoon garlic (grated)
  6. 1 teaspoon ginger (grated)
  7. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  8. 3 tablespoons cashews (ground)
  9. 1 cup tomato (pureed)
  10. 1 teaspoon coriander (ground)
  11. 3/4 teaspoon turmeric
  12. 1 tablespoon garam masala
  13. 1 tablespoon butter
  14. 3 tablespoons yogurt
  15. cilantro (chopped)

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a pan.
  2. Add the paneer and fry until golden brown on all sides.
  3. Let the paneer soak in warm water for 30 minutes.
  4. Melt the butter in a pan.
  5. Add the onion and saute until brown.
  6. Add the garlic and ginger and saute until fragrant, about a minute.
  7. Add the chili powder, cashews, tomatoes, coriander, turmeric and garam masala and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the paneer and simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and yogurt.

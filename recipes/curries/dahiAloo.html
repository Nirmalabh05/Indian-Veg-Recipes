Dhai Aloo

Dahi aloo is a very popular Indian curry, it is potato based curry that is prepared with lots of curd or dahi and some mild spices.

Ingredients

  1. Potato or aloo – 3, big (If using baby potatoes, use about 10)
  2. Onion – 1 small (finely chopped)
  3. Cloves – 2
  4. Cinnamon – small piece
  5. Garam masala powder – 2 tsp
  6. Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  7. Coriander or dhania powder – 1tbsp
  8. Amchur powder or dry mango powder – 1 tsp
  9. Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  10. Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  11. Yogurt or curd or dahi – 1 cup (well beaten) + 1/2 cup water
  12. Coriander leaves – to garnish
  13. Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  14. Green chili – 3 (slit)

Method

  1. First, cook the potatoes till they are completely soft. Peel them and cut them into bite size pieces.
  2. Fry them in 1 tsp oil till it turns into light golden color. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the remaining oil and let it heat.
  4. Add the mustard seeds, cloves, cinnamon and green chili. Let them pop.
  5. Add the ginger garlic paste and onions. Fry for few minutes.
  6. Add the coriander powder, dry mango powder, turmeric and red chili powder. Fry till the masala is well cooked. Remove from heat and add the well beaten curd.
  7. Add the coriander powder, dry mango powder, turmeric and red chili powder. Mix well and then add the potatoes, garam masala powder and coriander leaves.
  8. Adjust the seasoning and add the pickle. Give a good mix and serve warm.

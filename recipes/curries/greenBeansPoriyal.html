Green Beans Poriyal

South Indian Green Beans Poriyal Recipe - This dish will add a splash of colour to any table! It take so less time to make and takes the humble green beans to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  1. 250 gms french beans
  2. 2 tbsp oil
  3. 1 tsp mustard seeds
  4. 1 onion finely chopped
  5. 6-7 curry leaves
  6. 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  7. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  8. 1/4 cup fresh grated coconut
  9. salt to taste

Method

  1. Trim the edges of the beans and cut them into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a non stick pan and add the mustard seeds till they begin to splutter.
  3. Now throw in the curry leaves and onion. Lightly fry till couple of minutes.
  4. Toss in the cut beans and cook on low flame covered with the lid. (You can sprinkle some water here if you wish. I like to cook without the water on simmer. The steam helps with the cooking on itself and I feel the taste is much better this way)
  5. Cook till the beans have changed their color and cooked through.
  6. Add in the turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt and grated coconut. Stir well and cook for about 2-3 minutes more. Do not overcook after the coconut is added. It is just enough to dry up the water from the fresh coconut, which gives a nice crunchy taste to it. Switch off the flame.
  7. Serve hot with some hot rice and dal.
